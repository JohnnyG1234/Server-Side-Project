About:

New England Bikes, LLC is a subsidiary of Bicycles USA, Inc. The company is dedicated to producing high quality bicycles that hold their value over time. Our bikes are constructed in accordance with industry standards. Though we are here to make simple and affordable bicycles for all, we are also here to promote road saftey. Bike riding is fun for all, whether you are a casual bike rider or a professional bicycle racer. What we all share is the awareness of how critical it is to properly handle a bike and maintain saftey at all times.
